About Thai Cuisine


Thai Cuisine was found in 1998 by chef Wanida Harris who cooked for friends and neighbors from her small kitchen during festivals and celebrations. Today, Thai Cuisine celebrated 25 years of authentic Thai food, made from scratch Thai cooking.
